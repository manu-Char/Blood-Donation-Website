About BloodLink

Connecting Donors
With Patients In Need

BloodLink is a community-driven blood donation management platform dedicated to bridging the gap between voluntary blood donors and patients who urgently need blood.

We organize and manage blood donation camps across the city, making it easy for donors to find nearby camps, register, and contribute to saving lives.

🎯

Our Mission

Promote voluntary blood donation and ensure timely blood availability for all patients.

🤝

Community First

We work with local hospitals and organizations to organize regular donation camps.

🔒

Safe & Verified

All camps are organized under medical supervision with certified healthcare professionals.
